About us

OUR CHOCOLATE

From women for women!

Palette is a chocolate collection that gathers influence from a variety of bold women. We strive for high quality nourishing delicious experiences.

We use a palette of stimulating, natural, unique ingredients in our bars to reflect a woman's individuality and what she embodies for herself.

We aspire to create a fulfilling experience with a chocolate bar that will grant you moments of indulgence.


Our Flavors

We handcraft each of our chocolate bars with natural, dried fruits, spices and nuts, making a total of 10 delicious flavors.

Banana & Cinnamon

Nostalgic, warming cinnamon and homey banana keep you company with this comforting chocolate bar. Relax and cozy up!

Almond, Pecan, Pistachio & Salt

When you’re feeling nutty, it's not so hard to take a bite of this chocolatey treat of deliciousness. Almonds, pecans and pistachios. Go Nuts!

Strawberry & Cashew

Summery strawberry and creamy cashew plunge into a romance in this sweet and passionate chocolate bar. Let yourself fall in love!

Raspberry & Cherry

Bring out a sultry aroma of luscious cherry with a zing of raspberry to get you started for a sensual night. Be prepared to take a bite of this sexy chocolate!

Honey, Oats & Dates

The invigorating combination of honey, oats and dates in this bar is the perfect way to start your day and get ready for the world. The world better get ready for you!

Apricot & Blueberry

Rich apricot and powerful blueberry combine in a delicious bar that understands the beauty of small things. Don’t stop enjoying the simple pleasures in life!

Mango & Ginger

Fun, spunky mango with a dash of bold, spicy ginger makes this bar an exhilarating experience. Don’t miss out on the mango party!

Orange & Salt

A refreshing burst of orange and salt make a mix that brings out the determination and courage in those who prefer this bar. Embrace your bravery!

Pineapple & Coconut

Tangy pineapple and tropical coconut take you back to those breezy beach days laying under the palm trees. Lay back and enjoy!

Lemon & Lavander

A fragrant scent of lavender and a hint of citrus. Simple yet powerful, an elegant combination. A bite, a timeless moment. Make it yours!
